Tenacity® - A Perfect Way to Re-Energize Your Joints

Cell-based therapy is a major breakthrough in treating orthopedic conditions. Early degenerative osteoarthritis, sports injuries, and tennis elbow have been effectively treated with umbilical cord-derived stem cells. In patients with no-option joint disorders, baby stem cells have shown great promise.

GMP Standard Allogenic Cell Products Provider

Prasad Research Foundation derives Hematopoietic Stem Cells and Mesenchymal Stromal Cells from the umbilical cord with the highest quality and compliance.

Tenacity®: Re-Energize Your Joints!

Tenacity®, a product developed by Prasad Research Foundation, is a high-quality stem cell-based product focused on re-energizing joints and relieving pains in the joints. Prasad Hospitals has developed a proprietary administration module for Tenacity®. The physician can administer into the joints as per the protocol. Tenacity® reduces the pain in the joints and improves the overall mobility.

What is the best source for long-lasting stem cells?
