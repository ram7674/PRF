About Us
Aims: Our aim is to ensure safe regenerative therapeutic approaches in difficult disease conditions.
Objectives:

To derive and develop safe regenerative medicine products which can be effectively applied in dermatology and pulmonology.

To perform clinical research in the field of regenerative medicine and to develop domain expertise in research and therapy administration.

Contract Research Organization (CRO) for various clinical trials for National and International clinical research organizations.

To provide clinical research units such as Prasad Hospitals for ethical and evidence based clinical studies.

To establish incubator facilities for various cell repository and develop regenerative medicine products with all regulatory compliances.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our Vision is to pursue clinically relevant research on Regenerative proteome and transcriptomics with a potential therapeutic application in Dermatology and Pulmonology and orthopaedics. Improving mother and child health is our primary goal which involves a dynamic and continuous medical education program through visionary leaders.

Our Mission

Our mission is to involve clinicians and scientists from various disciplines in clinical research and apply knowledge to innovate novel technologies in biomarker development and regenerative medicine especially in the fields of mother and child health, pulmonology and orthopaedics.
